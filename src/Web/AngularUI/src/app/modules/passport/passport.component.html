Steps to Apply for Documents

1. Select the document type you would like to apply for


2. Type in the quanity of the selected document that you want


3. Select the Delevery mode you the document to be delivered to you.


4. Fill the remaining fields and afterwards click on 'click to make payment'.


5. After payment you, will be sent an email of your transaction.


6. You can now click on requested document to see the status of the document you applied for

To ensure that your payment is successful:
1. click on your request status from your portal.
2. The number of applications and their corresponding status will be displayed.
3. If the status is showing as “not paid”, click on the small blue icon at the right end corner of the application to complete the payment process

PLEASE NOTE: Email applications are not intended to be printed
